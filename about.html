Our Story

Driven by passion. Defined by perfection.

The Velocity Standard

Founded in 2012 by automotive enthusiasts who demanded perfection, Velocity Detailing Studio was born out of a desire for unparalleled vehicle preservation.

We don't just wash cars; we perform surgical aesthetic correction. Utilizing the finest compounds, highest-grade ceramics, and self-healing protective films, we ensure your investment maintains its showroom luster for years to come.

"Our mission is simple: to make every vehicle that leaves our bay look better than the day it was built."
